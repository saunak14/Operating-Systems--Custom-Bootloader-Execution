Implementing bootloader programs using Bochs (BIOS emulator)


First install Bochs-2.6.9.exe

Module 1: Printing "Hello World" by pushing characters onto register:
Step 1: Open Bochs
Step 2: In 'Edit Options', double click on 'Disk & Boot'
Step 3: In 'Type of floppy drive' select '3.5" 1.44M' (as our floppy size is 1.44 MB)
Step 4: In 'First floggy image/device' browse to 'Module 1' folder and select 'floppy.img'
Step 5: In 'Type of floppy media' select '1.44 M'
Step 6: Check 'Write Protection'
Step 7: Change 'Status' to 'Inserted'
Step 8: Now open 'Boot Options' tab and check the 'Skip Floppy Boot Signature Check'
Step 9: Press on OK
Step 10: Press the 'Start' Button under the 'simulation' header.
Step 11: When the screen prompts 'Press F12' press it
Step 12: The screen will show "Hello World" after a few seconds
